History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small local tech meetup and has grown into one of the largest technology conferences in the world, attracting innovators and enthusiasts from every corner of the globe.

Mission

TechCon 2024 aims to inspire and educate technology professionals, students, and enthusiasts by providing a platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and hands-on workshops.

Past Speakers

  • Elon T. Musk - Delivered keynote on AI innovations in 2015.
  • Grace Hopper - Shared insights on software development best practices in 2018.
  • Satya Nadella - CEO of Microsoft, visionary in cloud computing.
